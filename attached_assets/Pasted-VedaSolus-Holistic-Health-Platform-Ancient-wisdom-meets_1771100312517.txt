VedaSolus — Holistic Health Platform

Ancient wisdom meets modern science. Your complete wellness ecosystem.

Tagline: The world's first platform bridging Ayurveda, Traditional Chinese Medicine, and Western health science — powered by AI, secured by blockchain principles, and designed for real people.

Owner: Jason Andrews (100% ownership, all revenue flows through Orbit Financial Hub)

What It Does

VedaSolus is a full-stack holistic health platform that lets users track their wellness, get AI-powered coaching, connect with certified practitioners, and learn from centuries of Eastern healing traditions — all through a stunning, modern interface.

Core Capabilities

Personal Health Tracking — Log sleep patterns, diet/nutrition, exercise routines, and heart rate data with visual charts and trend analysis via Recharts.

Ayurvedic Dosha Analysis — Personalized Ayurvedic constitution assessment with tailored routine recommendations based on your unique Vata/Pitta/Kapha balance.

AI Wellness Coach — Conversational AI assistant powered by OpenAI with ElevenLabs voice synthesis for natural spoken guidance.

Health Passport — A sovereign, portable health identity with blockchain-verified credentials and QR code sharing.

Practitioner Marketplace — Browse, filter, and connect with certified holistic practitioners. Includes a lead capture system with inquiry forms for potential practitioners to join the platform.

Secure Messaging — Private communication between users and practitioners with real-time chat interface.

Meditation & Mindfulness — Guided meditation sessions with various techniques and session logging.

Wisdom Library — Educational content covering Ayurveda, TCM, herbalism, breathwork, and more.

Community Tribes — Social spaces organized by wellness philosophy (Paleo Ancestral, Vipassana Sitters, Biohacker Collective) with posting and engagement.

Health Records Management — Upload, manage, and verify medical documents with provider connection support.

Diet & Nutrition — Meal logging with Ayurvedic recipe recommendations tailored to your dosha.

Exercise Tracking — Workout logging with Qigong/movement practice integration.

Sleep Analysis — Detailed sleep pattern tracking with quality scoring.

Business & Admin Features

Stripe Payments — Subscription checkout and practitioner payment processing with webhook-based revenue sync.

Orbit Staffing Integration — Automated syncing of doctors, contractors, employees, timesheets, certifications, and revenue to Orbit Financial Hub.

Command Center — PIN-gated admin dashboard for platform-wide control across all modules.

Admin Dashboard — Content moderation queue, user management, and compliance reporting.

Developer Dashboard — Real-time analytics, system health KPIs, roadmap tracking, and developer console tools.

First-Party Analytics — Custom-built session tracking, page views, and event analytics — no third-party trackers.

SEO Management — Per-route SEO configuration with admin CRUD interface.

Business Plan Portal — Interactive business strategy view with milestones, financial projections, and team structure.

Partner Portal — Partnership opportunities and integration details.
